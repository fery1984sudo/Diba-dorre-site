Delivery & Returns

We offer delivery across a selection of EU countries within 2–4 working days. The flat delivery rate is 10 euros.

Returns & Exchanges

If your item is faulty or incorrect, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or replacement. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Return postage is the responsibility of the sender unless the product is faulty or damaged.
